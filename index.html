ABOUT US

VideoStar is a team of young creative Photographers, Videographers, Cinematographers and Dronographers specializing in curating stories for a lifetime. We've been in business for 35+ years and we believe in understanding the needs, desires and requirements of our clients to curate tailor-made, handcrafted films of top quality. We specialize in pre-wedding and wedding shoots.
